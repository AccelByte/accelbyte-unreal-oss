Detailed Description

Store, purchases, entitlements, and virtual economy.

Commerce interfaces handle in-game economy and monetization:

  • In-game store and catalog browsing
  • Item purchasing and checkout
  • Entitlement management (ownership verification)
  • Virtual currency (wallet) operations
  • Platform purchase sync (Steam, Epic, PlayStation, Xbox)

Related Interfaces:

  • FOnlineStoreInterfaceV2AccelByte - Store and catalog
  • FOnlinePurchaseInterfaceAccelByte - Purchase operations
  • FOnlineEntitlementsInterfaceAccelByte - Entitlement management
  • FOnlineWalletInterfaceAccelByte - Wallet operations (legacy)
  • FOnlineWalletV2InterfaceAccelByte - Wallet operations (new)
