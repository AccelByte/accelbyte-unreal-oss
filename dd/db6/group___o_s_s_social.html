Detailed Description

Friends, chat, presence, parties, and social interactions.

Social interfaces enable multiplayer social features:

  • Friends list management (add, remove, accept, reject, block)
  • Real-time chat and messaging
  • Party system (create, join, invite, kick)
  • User presence and status
  • Groups and communities
  • Recent players
